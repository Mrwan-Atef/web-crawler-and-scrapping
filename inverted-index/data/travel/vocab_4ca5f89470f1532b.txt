Chinatown the CBD Singapore Asia Top choice in Chinatown the CBD Login Save Delve into Chinatown s gritty cacophonous backstory at the immersive Chinatown Heritage Centre Occupying several levels of a associations to the district s notorious opium dens It s an evocative place digging well beneath modern Chinatown s touristy veneer The museum is physically very small so aim to arrive just after
