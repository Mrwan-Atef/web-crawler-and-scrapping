planet Framed by endless cobalt skies it stands remote at the western edge of Australia s massive expanse Northwest across the Indian Ocean the Asian megacity of Jakarta is closer than Melbourne or Sydney to Perth s east This geographic exile has instilled its population with confidence and verve The economic benefits of Australia s resources boom continue to inspire central Perth s ongoing urban makeover while a creative and entrepreneurial spirit drives the reinvention and renewal of inner suburbs and the nearby port town of Fremantle This whistlestop weekend itinerary will steer you
