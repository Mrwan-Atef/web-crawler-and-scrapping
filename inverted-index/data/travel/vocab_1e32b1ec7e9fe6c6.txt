Top choice in Malta Login Save The Hypogeum from the Greek meaning underground is a subterranean necropolis discovered during building work in To visit is to step into a mysterious and silent world Its halls chambers and passages immaculately hewn out of the rock cover some sq metres it is thought to date from around to BC and an estimated bodies may have been interred here Note that prebooking online is essential try to book around three months before your visit The ancient workers mimicked built masonry in carving out these underground chambers and exploited the rock s natural weaknesses and strengths to carve out the spaces by hand and create a safe underground structure Carbon dioxide exhaled by visiting tourists did serious damage to the delicate limestone walls of the burial chambers and it was closed to the public for years up to mid It has been restored with Unesco funding the microclimate is now strictly controlled and visitor numbers to the site are limited per tour
