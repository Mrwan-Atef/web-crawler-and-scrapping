Caribbean land known as the Virgin Islands Despite its small size square miles St John s wide range of rainfall and exposure give it surprising variety More than subtropical plant species grow in areas from moist high elevation forests to desertlike terrain to mangrove swamps among them mangoes soursops turpentine trees wild tamarind century plants and sea grapes Around the island live the fringing coral reefs beautiful complex and exceedingly fragile communities of plants and animals which St John s famous beaches depend upon In Columbus sighted the large assemblage of islands and cays and named it after St Ursula s legendary virgins Since then Spain France Holland England Denmark and the United States have controlled various islands at different times The Danes began colonization
