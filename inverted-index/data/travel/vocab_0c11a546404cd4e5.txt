beautifully curated museums What s on display is only a sliver of the objects the museum owns but the selected items are stunning The museum was heavily damaged in January in a car bomb attack on nearby police headquarters but after extensive renovations was finally reopened in early To the right as you enter are architectural details frescoes carved plaster so fine it looks like lace an crucified Christ To the left pieces are grouped by function and medium medical tools astrolabes some breathtaking carpets illuminated Qurans and even headstones The museum is due west from Bab Zuweila
