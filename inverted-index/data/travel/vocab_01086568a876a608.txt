over the Topatopa Mountains Fotosearch Getty Images Los Angeles holds a special place in the hearts of both locals and visitors thanks to its sprawling panoramic mountain views and breathtaking blue oceans Venturing beyond the urban landscape and into the surrounding area leads to a treasure trove of endless opportunities Just a short drive outside of La La Land will transport you to another world You ll find food and wine to delight you in Santa Barbara and a taste of small town Europe in Solvang Whether you re a local seeking a break from the bustling city or a visitor hoping to soak in the surroundings of Los Angeles these unique Southern California day trips guarantee memorable and one of a kind experiences to rejuvenate your spirit Advertisement Downtown Santa Barbara L Paul Mann Shutterstock Discover the Spanish colonial beauty of Santa Barbara Travel time hour and minutes Santa Barbara is full of natural beauty and cultural richness wrapped in a relaxed coastal lifestyle The town reflects its Spanish colonial heritage with Mediterranean style buildings and red tile roofs â the perfect complement to the Santa Ynez Mountains in the background Foodies and wine enthusiasts will love a trip to Santa Barbara for its near limitless offerings Start your day visiting CajÃ Coffee Roasters and order one of the signature and very Instagrammable drinks such as the Sugar Wood or Belarosa The tacos at La Super Rica are another must taste â there s a reason why Julia Child called this her favorite taco joint â and Bibi Ji is a local go to for its friendly atmosphere and delicious twist on modern Indian cuisine You also can t miss a wine tasting while in Santa Barbara If you re not venturing past downtown visit Grassini Family Vineyards tasting room for a estate flight and Salt Cave next door home to the largest Himalayan salt cave in North America A minute meditation session is held at the latter each hour guaranteed to leave you feeling relaxed and renewed A stroll down State St will see you end at Funk Zone to explore more tasting rooms cafes galleries and shops For culture and architecture lovers spend some time at the Old Mission where you can picnic in the beautiful rose garden The Santa Barbara Museum of Natural History is also close by If you re spending the night or leaving later in the evening try to catch a show at the Santa Barbara Bowl The spectacular outdoor amphitheater was built in and has panoramic ocean sunset views How to get to Santa Barbara from Los Angeles With no traffic it takes minutes on US N to travel the miles from Santa Barbara from Los Angeles Alternatively opt for the Amtrak train The gorgeous coastal ride starts from Union Station in Los Angeles and takes just over two and a half hours to arrive in downtown Santa Barbara Old Main street in Solvang s historic downtown travelview Shutterstock Indulge in Solvang s rich Danish wine country Travel time hours and minutes Advertisement Looking for a local European style getaway Visit Solvang the Danish capital of America Start by strolling or biking along Copenhagen Dr and indulge in Danish delicacies like aebleskiver pancake balls while sampling wine On the corner of Copenhagen Dr and Alisal Rd is Solvang Bakery While the name may not be familiar their work is This is the bakery responsible annually for the Kardashian family s lavish gingerbread houses In keeping with the holiday theme if the idea of year round Christmas is exciting stop by Jule Hus and stock up on some handcrafted Scandinavian ornaments While a day trip is doable you may want to spend more time in Solvang to get a feel for its vineyards and downtown tasting rooms including Cali Love which features karaoke and DJ sets during your tasting Visit the Hans Christian Anderson Museum dedicated to The Little Mermaid author if you re a fan and definitely take advantage of the town s trolley tour How to get to Solvang from Los Angeles Leave early to avoid traffic and make the most of your day Follow US N toward Santa Barbara for minutes before veering off to CA W San Marcos Pass Rd for the rest of the way Driving the mile journey is necessary as public transport will take more than five hours Balboa Island near Newport Beach Cavan Images Shutterstock Explore the beauty of Newport Beach s coastal delights Travel time hour Newport Beach boasts extensive water and land activities as well as entertainment for singles couples and families With miles of coastline Newport Beach is known for being one of the largest small craft harbors in the US boasting approximately privately owned yachts and sailboats When visiting Newport Beach allocate some time to visit Balboa Island a manmade island built between and To get there catch the ferry on foot or stay in your car for the short three minute ride Ticket prices range between and each way and the ferry runs until midnight While there a Balboa bar or frozen banana from the iconic Sugar n Spice is a must Back in Newport Beach relax by the ocean at Crystal Cove State Park The entrance is down a flight of stairs past Shake Shack an iconic style diner not to be confused with the fast food chain Order your shake burger and fries to go and eat down on the beach or for a more hearty feed enjoy upscale Mexican at Javier s or Italian classics at Mama D s â there will be a line but it s worth the wait To get a better view of the ocean consider hiking the Back Bay Loop Trail You can enter this multi purpose trail from a number of points and hike anywhere from to miles Dogs are welcome as long as they re leashed How to get to Newport Beach from Los Angeles With no traffic Newport Beach is a quick minute mile drive from Los Angeles Heading down S merge onto I S and continue straight Taking public transport is not advisable as it requires multiple modes of transportation and will take more than three hours Spa in Ojai is the perfect place for a spa day getaway Stefan Von Imhof Getty Images Restore your spirit and get grounded in Ojai Travel time hour and minutes Ojai OH hi is found in a small valley north of Ventura and east of Santa Barbara The Ojai Valley lies in a geometric vortex zone described by Native Americans as an energy field rising from the earth s core due to the pull from the Topatopa Mountains The city derives its name from the Chumash word for moon nest and has bountiful amounts of culture history farm to table restaurants bakeries breweries and wineries Ojai s calming energy makes it a great place for those looking to reconnect with themselves and others in a relaxing environment To treat yourself visit the Garden Spa at the Lavender Inn and opt for a massage foot bath and customized herbal exfoliation The spa even does prayers and uses sacred oils After the spa head downtown for lunch at Harvest Moon or Ojai Rotie order the chicken Continue your grounding day at Bart s Books which claims to be the largest outdoor bookstore in the world and soak in the culture at Dan Schultz Fine Art Gallery The Ojai Olive Oil Company s tasting is also a must do Sunday is a great day to visit Ojai to enjoy the weekly farmer s market How to get to Ojai from Los Angeles The mile journey should take minutes along US N and CA N without traffic Public transport is possible via Amtrak from Union Station and a changeover to a bus in Ventura a journey that takes approximately three hours Aerial view of Torrance Beach The Desert Photo Shutterstock Eat your way through Torrance the ramen capital of Southern California Travel time minutes Torrance is part of the South Bay region of Los Angeles County and prides itself on being a city of hidden gems The area is a haven for food lovers particularly those partial to huge flavors and international cuisines Outside of Honolulu Torrance is the home to the largest concentration of Japanese residents in the US and it s been dubbed the ramen capital of Southern California Local ramen favorites include Matsui Hakata Ikkousha Ramen Ko Ryu Ramen take the spicy challenge if you dare and the Shin Sen Gumi drive thru When you re all ramen ed out Torrance has more than other eateries including the popular Rascals Teriyaki Grill King s Hawaiian Bakery and Restaurant and Piccini Italian Takeout Beyond food community is at the forefront for Torrance locals The city hosts an annual Summer Nights festival with musicals movies and live music playing at the Wilson Park Amphitheater between June and September A visit to the Torrance Art Museum is also a must for contemporary art lovers Admission is free and the galleries and displays are frequently updated Being only minutes from both LAX and SoFi Stadium Torrance is the perfect central location for those looking to spend a few hours before a flight or stadium event Torrance Beach is also nearby with a mile stretch of uncrowded coastline nestled between Redondo Beach and Malaga Cove How to get to Torrance from Los Angeles Getting to Torrance is a quick minutes from Los Angeles miles via I S Alternatively hop on the J Line Metro from downtown and change for a bus at the Harbor Gateway Transit Center The trip should take a little less than
