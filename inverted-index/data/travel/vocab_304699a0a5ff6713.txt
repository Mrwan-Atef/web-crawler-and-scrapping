America s Beach Towns National Geographic Traveler Encinitas California Just half an hour s drive north of San Diego Encinitas s shores draw surfers and sunset beachcombers Staff Tip When visiting the beaches of Encinitas make time to swing by Pannikin Coffee Tea Delicious coffee and coffee related beverages oversize gluten free muffins a yummy breakfast burrito and local art are just some of its perks Started in at this location it s a mainstay of San Diego coffee culture Another delicious local joint is Swami s Cafe on Hwy Enjoy a fresh juice and a hearty brunch surrounded by Encinitas
