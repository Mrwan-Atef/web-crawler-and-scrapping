promontory at the entrance to Santiago harbor southwest of the city The stupendous views from the upper terrace take in the wild western ribbon of Santiago s coastline backed by the velvety Sierra Maestra Multilingual guides provide invaluable historical background and color be sure to tip The fort was designed in by famous Italian military engineer Juan Bautista Antonelli who also designed La
