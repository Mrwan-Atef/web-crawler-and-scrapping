Upolu Login Save The name of this sq km national park means from the coast to the mountaintop There are some superb if rough hikes to be had here A trail six hours return winds through thick jungle to Pe ape a Cave a large lava tube inhabited by pe ape a swiftlets bring a torch From here the hardcore can continue along a heavily overgrown trail to Ofa Waterfall three days return For a less intense tramp the Ma Tree Walk ends at a gigantic rainforest tree with huge buttress roots For the longer walks a guide such as Eti from SamoaOnFoot is essential At the park s western boundary a bumpy unsealed access road open to leads to the magnificently rugged O Le Pupu Lava Coast where a rocky coastal trail leads along lava cliffs the bases of which are constantly harassed by enormous waves
