choice in Bangkok Login Save Named after the Buddha s birthplace in Nepal Lumbini Lumphini Park is central Bangkok s largest and most popular park Its hectares are home to an artificial lake surrounded by broad well tended lawns wooded areas walking paths and startlingly large resident monitor lizards to complement the shuffling citizens It s the best outdoor escape from Bangkok without actually leaving town The park was originally a royal reserve but in Rama VI King Vajiravudh r declared it a public space A statue of the founding emperor can be found at the southwestern entrance of the park One of the best times to visit is early morning or late evening when the air is relatively fresh or pleasantly balmy and legions of Thai Chinese are practising t ai chi or doing their best to mimic the aerobics instructor or doing the half run half walk version of jogging that makes a lot of sense in oppressive humidity There are paddleboats for lovers playgrounds for the kids and
