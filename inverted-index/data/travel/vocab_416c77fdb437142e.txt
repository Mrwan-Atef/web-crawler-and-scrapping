Caribbean St Lucia Login Save Looking like something off the surface of the moon the Sulphur Springs are saddled with the unfortunate tagline of being the world s only drive in volcano The reality is far from the garish description There isn t a classic crater or a cauldron of magma to check out but it s still an awe inspiring place Stinky pools of boiling mud are observed from platforms surrounded by vents releasing clouds of sulfur gas Kids will love it There is a thermally heated river that has been dammed to form four small pools where visitors can relax in the mineral rich waters
