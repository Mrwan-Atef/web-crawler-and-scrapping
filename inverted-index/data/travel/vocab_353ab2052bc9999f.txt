Location American Samoa Established October Size acres land marine For some years the people of Polynesia s oldest culture have been keenly attuned to their island environment holding it to be precious and managing it communally The name they gave their land reflects their attitude Samoa means sacred earth Located roughly miles southwest of Hawaii American Samoa a United States territory comprises ten volcanic islands five inhabited and two coral atolls one inhabited In Congress authorized the land for a national park In Samoan chiefs agreed to sign a year lease that enables the National Park Service to manage an area of rain forest beach and coral reef on three islands Samoans help manage the park and their villages offer a few guest facilities ask the park about its unique homestay program The park protects hundreds of plant species in five distinct rain forest communities lowland montane coast ridge and cloud It is the only such rain forest on American soil Among the fauna visitors can see are tropical birds and the endangered flying fox a fruit bat with the wingspan of a barn owl On Tutuila American Samoa s largest island lofty volcanic ridges overlook the deep blue waters of Pago Pago Harbor Except for a few villages and the scenic drive that skirts the harbor and the dramatic southern coastline there is little level land Atop this crumbled terrain and plunging steeply toward the sea on the island s northern side lies the park area about acres of land and some acres of ocean Parkland on Ta u the easternmost island encompasses about acres including Lata Mountain American Samoa s highest peak and acres offshore Unforgettable is the panoramic view from the cloud forest toward the rugged cliffs of the southern coast Small remote Ofu Island includes
