originally hails from Southwest England but realized early on that he felt equally at home wherever a long haul flight long distance bus or long stint at the roadside waiting for a lift would take him Now a Mid Wales based travel writer specializing in adventure and culinary travel especially in the UK outdoors Scandinavia and South America he has been writing for Lonely Planet since In that time he has authored or contributed to over Lonely Planet travel guidebooks and reference books He s happiest very far from the nearest road or WiFi connection either hiking biking or trail running He also writes for publications including BBC Travel the Telegraph and Adventure com and in other projects has written two novels and the first ever widlife watching walk book to accompany the BBC Springwatch
