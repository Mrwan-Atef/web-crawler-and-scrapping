Cathedral Sofia Bulgaria Europe Top choice in Sofia Login Save One of the symbols not just of Sofia but of Bulgaria itself this massive awe inspiring church was built between and in memory of the Russian soldiers who died fighting for Bulgaria s independence during the Russo Turkish War It is named in honour of a century Russian warrior prince Designed by Russian architect Alexander Pomerantsev the church was built in the neo Byzantine style favoured in Russia at the time and is adorned with mosaics and gold laden domes The cavernous incense scented interior is decorated with naturalistic though now rather faded murals pendulous chandeliers and elaborate onyx and alabaster thrones Visitors are welcome and there are daily services where you can hear evocative Orthodox
