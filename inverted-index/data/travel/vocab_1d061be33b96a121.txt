companions for nomadic communities who used them to catch rabbits and other small game While there s not much call for their skills in the desert these days they remain a beloved part of the Arabian Peninsula heritage with pure bred well behaved dogs fetching thousands of dirhams Many are bred to race and according to the Guinness Book of Records a saluki holds the record for four legged speed at
