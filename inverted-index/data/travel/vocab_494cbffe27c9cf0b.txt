is To Le Sua a smaller drier depression it s To Sua that is the star of the show more akin to a giant sinkhole than a trench its sheer green draped rock walls plummet odd metres to the almost hallucinatory blue waters of the magnificent pool below Swimming access is via a precipitous but sturdy wooden ladder believe us it s worth the clamber When you ve had your fill of this enchanted waterhole take the short track to the wave battered cliffs The well groomed garden is a great spot for a picnic Gorgeous though it is here visitors still need to exercise caution swimming through the
