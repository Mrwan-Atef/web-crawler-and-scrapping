National Parks Best Road Trips in America Ultimate Guides Login Subscribe This Disco Diva Cruise Was So Popular They re Doing It Again Scott Laird April Travel News News Save Never can say goodbye I n the fall of Crystal Cruises brought a special entertainment act onboard Crystal Serenity on a sailing from Quebec City to Fort Lauderdale two time Grammy Award winning artist Gloria Gaynor best known for the disco anthems I Will Survive and Never Can Say Goodbye During that sailing which a Fodor s contributor found transformative Gaynor performed a special concert with a ten piece live band and surprise guests among her backup singers The next day a sea day Gaynor also participated in an intimate Q A session with passengers onboard The sailing proved so popular with passengers on the sailing that Crystal will be giving passengers another chance to sail with the disco legend and enjoy another intimate concert and Q A Passengers booking Crystal Serenity s October sailing from New York City will enjoy Gaynor s second performance onboard Crystal Performing aboard Crystal Serenity was an absolutely wonderful experience the warmth of the guests the elegance of the ship and the incredible energy made it truly special Gaynor said in a statement I am beyond thrilled to return this October for another unforgettable journey sharing my music and connecting with everyone once again Cruise line entertainment can be a hard needle to thread Cruise lines must choose live theatre acts that have broad enough appeal to fill a theatre onboard a ship with plenty of other distractions On the
