Login Save The former capital of the Zapotec people is today an illustrious ruin but for over a thousand years it served as the second largest ceremonial site in Mesoamerica after Teotihuacán Sitting atop a flattened hill miles west of present day Oaxaca Monte Albán s setting is spectacular The site is known for its expansive Gran Plaza ancient ball court excavated tombs astronomical observatory and uniquely its mysterious danzantes carved stone figures depicting what are thought to be dead or tortured prisoners of war At the entrance to the site are a good museum explanations in Spanish only
